About

Iron Man, Tony Stark, is a fictional character who appears in American comic books. He plays the character of a wealthy businessman and engineer. He experienced a severe chest injury when he was kidnapped. His kidnappers were forcing him to come up with a destructive weapon. Rather than creating the weapon, Tony Stark develops a powered suit of armor for the purpose of saving his life and escaping captivity. He then enhances his suit with weapons and other technological devices he created by use of his company known as the Stark Industries. He utilizes the suit and successive versions in protecting the world as Iron Man, while first hiding his real identity.
